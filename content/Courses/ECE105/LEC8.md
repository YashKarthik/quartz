- Relative motion.

- Velocity and other params depend on the frame of reference we observe from.

$x_{PA}$ is read as position of P in A's reference frame.

$$
\vec x_{PA} = \vec x_{PB} + \vec x_{BA}
$$
These are basically position vectors.
